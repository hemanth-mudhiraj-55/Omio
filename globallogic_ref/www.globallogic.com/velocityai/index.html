FAQs

Your questions about VelocityAI – Answered

Still have questions? We’re here to help you explore and engineer what’s next.

  • What is GlobalLogic VelocityAI?
    VelocityAI is GlobalLogic’s unified AI strategy and delivery ecosystem. It embeds governed intelligence across software development, enterprise workflows, and physical systems. Spanning AI-Powered SDLC, Enterprise AI, and Physical AI, VelocityAI moves organizations from experimentation to production-grade impact—engineering intelligence that is secure, observable, and built for scale.
  • How is VelocityAI different from traditional AI services?
    VelocityAI goes beyond model experimentation and automation pilots. It is designed for production deployment, with governance, security, and runtime observability embedded from day one. Rather than focusing solely on models, VelocityAI emphasizes agentic orchestration, enterprise integration, and Reliable AI principles—ensuring AI systems operate safely, measurably, and in alignment with business objectives.
  • What is Agentic AI and how does VelocityAI support it?
    Agentic AI refers to systems of AI agents that collaborate across workflows to sense, reason, and act within defined guardrails. VelocityAI enables Agentic AI through governed orchestration layers, reusable design patterns, and enterprise-grade runtime controls. This allows organizations to move from isolated bots to coordinated agent ecosystems that drive measurable business outcomes.
  • What does Reliable AI mean at GlobalLogic?
    Reliable AI is an engineering principle embedded across VelocityAI. It ensures AI systems are explainable, observable, and governed at runtime—especially in mission-critical and regulated environments. Reliable AI combines hybrid reasoning, policy enforcement, and human-in-the-loop oversight to reduce hallucinations, enforce compliance, and ensure AI behaves predictably in production.
  • How does VelocityAI enable AI in physical and edge environments?
    Through its Physical AI pillar, VelocityAI brings governed intelligence to real-world systems such as vehicles, factories, energy grids, and embedded devices. By combining edge-based reasoning, digital twin validation, and secure orchestration, GlobalLogic enables AI systems that can sense, decide, and act reliably in safety-critical environments without compromising control or compliance.
